OnChainFest

OnChainFest is a Web3 infrastructure that enables the organization of sports events, raffles, and concerts with full transparency, automation, and traceability through smart contracts and NFTs. OnChainFest makes event organization easier, safer, and transparent with Web3.

Soluciones

Our platform allows any organizer to create verifiable registrations, distribute prizes automatically, and issue NFT tickets to prevent fraud and scalping. Everything is managed from a user-friendly interface integrated with wallets and sponsors. NFT tickets, prizes and registration all in one Web3 platform.
