Music Events

We transform traditional ticketing by issuing NFT-based tickets that eliminate fraud, prevent overselling, and unlock collectible fan experiences. Each NFT ticket is verifiable on-chain, transferable between wallets, and can provide exclusive benefits like discounts, VIP access, or post-event content. Our solution enables the decentralization of ticket distribution points, empowering artists and organizers to sell tickets independently without relying on a single central system. This significantly reduces cloud infrastructure costs compared to traditional Web2 ticketing platforms that require heavy server usage and complex backend architectures. Organizers can also automatically split revenues between artists and partners and gain valuable data on ticket usage and resale patterns to optimize future editions.

Raffles

With OnChainFest, raffles become fully transparent and trustworthy. We use smart contracts and verifiable oracles to ensure provably fair and tamper-proof draws. Every ticket is linked to a unique wallet, and the draw is executed publicly with results stored on-chain. Prizes are automatically distributed, removing human error or favoritism. Ideal for fundraising events, brand activations, and secure audience engagement campaigns.
