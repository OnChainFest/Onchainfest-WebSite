OnChainFest

OnChainFest is a Web3 infrastructure that enables the organization of sports events, raffles, and concerts with full transparency, automation, and traceability through smart contracts and NFTs.

Sport Events

Create decentralized tournaments where entry fees, rebuys, and prize distribution are managed through smart contracts. All results are auditable and recorded immutably.

Music Events

Issue NFT tickets to avoid fraud and scalping. Track attendance, reward superfans, and share revenue transparently among artists, venues, and sponsors.

Raffles

Host verifiable raffles where winners are selected using on-chain randomness. Funds are locked, distributed instantly, and winners are published transparently.

$BRT’s - Bragging Rights Tokens

Issue NFTs to champions of tournaments. These collectible tokens can be shared or sold, immortalizing each player's victory in a limited digital edition.
